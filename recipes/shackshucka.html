Shackshucka

Freshly cooked Shackshucka

ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 red onions
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 orange pepper
  • 1 red chilli (deseeded)
  • 2 cans of red tomatos
  • 2 finely chopped garlic cloves
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp caster sugar

Make our easy shackshucka for a comforting brunch. Vary this dish by flavouring the simple tomato sauce with whatever you have to hand - curry powder, pesto or fresh herbs

The sauce can be frozen for one month
