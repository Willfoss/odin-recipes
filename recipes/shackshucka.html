Shackshucka

Freshly cooked Shackshucka

ingredients

Make our easy shackshucka for a comforting brunch. Vary this dish by flavouring the simple tomato sauce with whatever you have to hand - curry powder, pesto or fresh herbs

The sauce can be frozen for one month

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 red onions
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 orange pepper
  • 1 red chilli (deseeded)
  • 2 cans of red tomatos
  • 2 finely chopped garlic cloves
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  1. Add the onions, pepper, chilli and garlic to a pan and fry over a medium heat for 8-10 minutes or until soft
  2. Add in the chopped tomatoes and caster sugar and simmer over a low heat until the sauce thickens
  3. Use a back of a spoon to put four holes in the mixture. break the eggs and place them in these holes
  4. Place a lid on the pan and leave the eggs cook for 6-8 mins
